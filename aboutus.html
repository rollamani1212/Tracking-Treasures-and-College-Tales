About Us

This project proposes a solution to streamline the process of item retrieval within college campuses. The platform allows students to post detailed descriptions and images of their lost items along with their contact information. Individuals who find lost items can easily access the platform to identify and connect with the rightful owners.

This system aims to foster efficient communication and facilitate the prompt return of lost belongings, through the integration of user-friendly interfaces and robust backend functionalities. The proposed website offers a practical and accessible solution to mitigate the challenges associated with lost and found items on college campuses.
